
brief_title: Evaluation of Efficacy of Photodynamic Therapy in Basal Cell Carcinoma Sense3
official_title: Evaluation of Efficacy of Photodynamic Therapy in Basal Cell Carcinoma
brief_summary:
 The purpose of this study is to evaluate the efficacy of pdt in treatment of bcc and factors
that affect response rate.
inclusion_criteria:
 - Histologically proven BCCs
exclusion_criteria:
 - rodent ulcer - morphea type - ANA + - history of photosensitivity or photodermatosis or ingestion of phototoxic drugs during last month - pregnancy - breast feeding - age< 18 - history of another therapeutic intervention for bcc during last 6 months
