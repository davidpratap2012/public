
brief_title: Resuscitation Fluid Choice and Clinical Outcomes
official_title:  Association of Resuscitation Fluid Choice and Clinical Outcomes in Critically Ill Patients
brief_summary:
 This observational study evaluates the potential benefits, costs and clinical outcomes of albumin Meaning not listed over saline and other non-saline fluids in patients receiving large volume resuscitation.
inclusion_criteria:
 - Critically ill patient admitted to ICU
exclusion_criteria:
 - History of chronic dialysis and/or renal transplant - Baseline serum creatinine >= 4 mg/dl - Develop AKI or MA prior to LVR - Received OR fluids during LVR
